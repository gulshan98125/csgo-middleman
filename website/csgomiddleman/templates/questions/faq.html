Frequently asked questions

  • How to use the website?
  • First of all both the users have to register on this website.

    After that one of the user has to create a trade link from the dashboard page and share that link with the other user.

    The other user clicks on that link and after opening the link both the users are connected to trade.

    Website accept skins/keys first and then the paytm, so the skins/keys depositor has to submit first and after successful submission website asks for paytm from the other user.

    After both the users have submitted their respective items, website then sends the items respectively to the users i.e. user giving skins/keys gets the paytm and the user giving money gets the skins/keys
  • Where is the trade link?
  • Once you create a trade the tradelink is displayed at the top of the page or in the address bar
  • I submitted my skins/keys but the other user didn't complete the trade
  • In that case the items you submitted will be sent back to you after 20 minutes from the time you submitted them
  • I am getting HTTP 500 error on tradepage creation what does this mean?
  • This means that your inventory can't be loaded by the website. Check whether your inventory is loadable or not by going into your steam profile, if not then try again after some time.
  • The trade completed but I didn't receive my skins/keys or money
  • Keep calm, if there is any failure in the trade then your items or money is safe with us.

    Write us a support ticket about this matter with your screenshot of the accepted trade or sent paytm money.

    Screenshots are very useful to us for smooth workflow, if you forgot to take it then send us the 6 letters long trade id from your trade history.
  • Why some of my items are not displayed?
  • Only tradeable items are displayed. If none of your items are displayed then maybe its because they aren't tradable or you have a vac ban id.
